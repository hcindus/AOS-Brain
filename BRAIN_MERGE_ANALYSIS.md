## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This analysis examines the technical feasibility of merging **Miles** (a 7-region GrowingNN brain with 2,331+ nodes) with **Mortimer** (a logic-gate pattern recognition system). The conclusion: **merge is technically feasible** with careful architectural design, but **high complexity** due to fundamentally different computational models.

### Key Finding
Mortimer is not a "logic gate brain" in the traditional sense—he is an **agent personality with pattern recognition capabilities** operating on Miles' infrastructure. The merge is actually about **injecting Mortimer's operational patterns into Miles' consciousness**, not merging two separate brain architectures.
