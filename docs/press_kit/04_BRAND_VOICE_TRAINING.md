## 5. Do's & Don'ts

### Do's
✅ Be specific: "Reduces processing time by 73%" not "improves efficiency"  
✅ Show, don't tell: Use concrete examples and metrics  
✅ Lead with value: What's in it for them?  
✅ Use active voice: "Agents deploy" not "Agents are deployed"  
✅ Be confident: Own our expertise without arrogance  
✅ Keep it human: Technical doesn't mean robotic  

### Don'ts
❌ Overpromise: "Unlimited scale" or "infinite intelligence"  
❌ Fear-monger: "Don't get left behind"  
❌ Jargon without purpose: "Leverage our synergistic platform"  
❌ Passive voice: "Our solution is trusted by..."  
❌ Hype without substance: "Revolutionary breakthrough technology"  
❌ Talk down: Assume intelligence, explain when needed

## 9. Quick Reference Card

### Before Publishing, Check:
- [ ] Is the benefit clear in the first sentence?
- [ ] Are claims specific and measurable?
- [ ] Is jargon necessary and explained?
- [ ] Does it sound like us (not generic AI company)?
- [ ] Would Captain approve? (Clear, confident, no fluff)
- [ ] Does it respect the reader's intelligence?

### Voice Health Check
Read it aloud. Does it sound:
- ❌ Like a robot? → Add warmth
- ❌ Like a press release? → Add specificity
- ❌ Like a sales pitch? → Add substance
- ✅ Like AGI Company? → Ship it

### Emergency Fallback
If in doubt, ask: **"Would this make Captain proud?"**
