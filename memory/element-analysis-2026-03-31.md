### Section 1.3: Key Insights from Periodic Table Data

**Valence Pattern Analysis:**
- s-block: 2 columns (1-2), valence electrons in s orbital
- p-block: 6 columns (13-18), valence electrons in p orbital
- d-block: 10 columns (3-12), valence electrons in d orbital
- f-block: 14 elements, valence electrons in f orbital
- **Predicted g-block: 18 columns** - this is the major gap!

**Periodic Trends Observed:**
1. **Atomic Radius:** Decreases left to right, increases top to bottom
2. **Ionization Energy:** Increases left to right, decreases top to bottom
3. **Electronegativity:** Follows similar pattern to ionization energy
4. **Electron Affinity:** Generally increases toward upper right
5. **Metallic Character:** Decreases toward upper right
