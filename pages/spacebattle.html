🌌 Procedural Universe

Every grid cell (100x100x100) generates its own unique solar system with procedurally generated suns, planets, moons, and asteroid belts.

🚀 Voxel Ships

Fly customizable voxel spacecraft with distinct designs. Choose from nimble fighters, industrial Gordon-class ships, and deadly interceptors.

🤖 AI Combat

Battle intelligent enemy AI that chases, evades, and fires back. Dynamic difficulty scaling with wave progression.

💥 Physics Combat

Real collision detection - crash into asteroids, bounce off planets, or get destroyed by the sun's gravitational pull.

🛡️ Shields & Weapons

Regenerating shields, hull integrity, weapon heat management, and explosive particle effects create intense combat.

📱 Cross-Platform

Play with mouse/keyboard on desktop or touch controls on mobile. Seamless experience across all devices.
