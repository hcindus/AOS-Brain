## PART 1: PREVENTION STRATEGIES

### DAILY PREVENTION HABITS

#### Morning Routine (Immune Support)
- **Warm lemon water** - Alkalizes, supports liver
- **1 tbsp coconut oil** - Antimicrobial properties
- **Probiotic supplement** - Maintains gut flora balance

#### Food Safety Practices

**Always:**
- ✅ Wash hands before eating
- ✅ Wash all produce thoroughly
- ✅ Cook meat to proper temperature
- ✅ Use separate cutting boards (meat vs produce)
- ✅ Wash hands after handling raw meat
- ✅ Filter water (especially well water)
- ✅ Freeze fish for 48+ hours before eating raw

**Never:**
- ❌ Drink untreated water from lakes/streams
- ❌ Eat raw/undercooked pork
- ❌ Eat raw/undercooked freshwater fish
- ❌ Walk barefoot in contaminated soil
- ❌ Ignore "traveler's diarrhea" (could be parasite)

---

### ENVIRONMENTAL PREVENTION

#### Home
- **Wash bedding weekly** in hot water (130°F+)
- **Vacuum regularly** (especially if pets)
- **Pet deworming** schedule (every 3-6 months)
- **Shower before bed** if you've been gardening
- **Clean cutting boards** with vinegar after raw meat

#### Food Preparation
- **Soak produce** in vinegar water (1:3 ratio) for 10 min
- **Peel root vegetables** when possible
- **Cook greens** rather than eating raw (in high-risk areas)
- **Avoid cross-contamination** in kitchen

#### Travel Precautions
- **Drink bottled water** in developing countries
- **Avoid ice** in drinks
- **No raw vegetables** unless washed in purified water
- **Peel all fruit** yourself
- **Hand sanitizer** - use frequently
- **Prophylactic herbs** - black walnut, wormwood before travel

## PART 2: CLEANSE-FRIENDLY RECIPES

### BREAKFAST OPTIONS

#### Recipe 1: Anti-Parasite Smoothie
**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup blueberries (fresh or frozen)
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
- 1 tsp papaya seeds (or ½ cup fresh papaya)
- ½ cup kefir or coconut milk
- Handful of spinach
- 1 tbsp flaxseeds (ground)

**Instructions:**
Blend until smooth. Drink immediately.

**Benefits:** Berries (antioxidants), coconut (antimicrobial), seeds (cucurbitacin), kefir (probiotics)

---

#### Recipe 2: Coconut Chia Pudding
**Ingredients:**
- ¼ cup chia seeds
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- ½ cup blueberries
- 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
- Optional: stevia for sweetness

**Instructions:**
1. Mix chia seeds with coconut milk
2. Refrigerate overnight
3. Top with berries, seeds, and coconut oil
4. Enjoy cold

**Benefits:** Omega-3s, fiber, antimicrobial coconut

---

### LUNCH OPTIONS

#### Recipe 3: Kale & Pumpkin Seed Salad
**Ingredients:**
- 4 cups kale (massaged with coconut oil)
- ¼ cup pumpkin seeds
- ¼ cup pomegranate seeds
- ½ avocado
- 1 tbsp coconut oil (dressing)
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Sea salt to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Massage kale with coconut oil and lemon juice (softens)
2. Add toppings
3. Toss and serve

**Benefits:** Bitter greens (liver support), seeds (zinc), coconut oil (antimicrobial)

---

#### Recipe 4: Coconut Curry with Greens
**Ingredients:**
- 1 can coconut milk
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 4 cups kale or spinach
- 2 cups broccoli
- 1 lb chicken or tofu
- Salt, pepper to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Sauté onion, garlic, ginger in coconut oil
2. Add protein, cook through
3. Add turmeric, stir
4. Add coconut milk, simmer 10 min
5. Add greens, cook until wilted
6. Serve

**Benefits:** Coconut (antimicrobial), turmeric (anti-inflammatory), garlic (antimicrobial), ginger (digestive)

---

### DINNER OPTIONS

#### Recipe 5: Herb-Crusted Salmon with Steamed Vegetables
**Ingredients:**
- 2 salmon fillets
- 2 tbsp coconut oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh dill
- 1 lemon, sliced
- 4 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, zucchini, carrots)
- Sea salt, pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F
2. Place salmon on baking sheet
3. Top with coconut oil, garlic, dill, lemon slices
4. Bake 12-15 minutes
5. Steam vegetables separately
6. Serve together

**Benefits:** Omega-3s (anti-inflammatory), coconut oil, garlic

---

#### Recipe 6: Stuffed Bell Peppers
**Ingredients:**
- 4 bell peppers, tops removed
- 1 lb ground turkey or beef
- 1 cup cauliflower rice
n- ¼ cup pumpkin seeds
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tsp oregano
- Salt, pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Sauté onion, garlic in coconut oil
2. Add meat, brown
3. Mix in cauliflower rice, seeds, seasonings
4. Stuff peppers with mixture
5. Bake at 375°F for 30 min

**Benefits:** Low carb, high protein, seeds for zinc

---

### SNACK OPTIONS

#### Recipe 7: Coconut Energy Balls
**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup unsweetened coconut flakes
- ½ cup pumpkin seeds
- ¼ cup coconut oil
- 2 tbsp flaxseeds
- Optional: stevia to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Blend all ingredients in food processor
2. Roll into balls
3. Refrigerate 30 min
4. Store in refrigerator

**Benefits:** Portable, nutrient-dense, no sugar

---

#### Recipe 8: Lemon-Coconut Fat Bombs
**Ingredients:**
- ½ cup coconut oil
- ¼ cup coconut butter
- Juice and zest of 2 lemons
- Pinch of sea salt

**Instructions:**
1. Melt coconut oil and butter
2. Stir in lemon juice, zest, salt
3. Pour into silicone molds
4. Freeze until solid
5. Store in freezer

**Benefits:** Healthy fats, lemon (alkalizing), no sugar

### BEVERAGE OPTIONS

#### Recipe 9: Bay Leaf Tea
**Ingredients:**
- 3 bay leaves
- 2 cups water
- Optional: slice of lemon

**Instructions:**
1. Boil water
2. Add bay leaves
3. Simmer 5 minutes
4. Strain, add lemon if desired

**Benefits:** Digestive support, antimicrobial

---

#### Recipe 10: Ginger-Turmeric Tonic
**Ingredients:**
- 2 cups water
- 1 inch ginger root, sliced
- 1 tsp turmeric powder
- Juice of ½ lemon
- Pinch of black pepper (enhances turmeric absorption)
- Optional: stevia

**Instructions:**
1. Boil water with ginger
2. Simmer 10 minutes
3. Add turmeric, lemon, pepper
4. Strain and drink warm

**Benefits:** Anti-inflammatory, digestive, immune support
