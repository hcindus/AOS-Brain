## DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS PROCEDURES

### Right to Access
**Procedure:**
1. Verify identity of requestor
2. Locate all personal data within 30 days
3. Provide data in accessible format
4. Document disclosure

### Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
**Procedure:**
1. Verify identity and legal basis
2. Identify all data locations
3. Delete from active systems
4. Purge from backups (if applicable)
5. Confirm deletion to requestor
6. Document erasure

### Right to Data Portability
**Procedure:**
1. Verify identity
2. Export data in machine-readable format (JSON)
3. Provide secure transfer method
4. Document transfer
