## BLACK WALNUT HULL TINCTURE PREPARATION

### Ingredients
- Black walnut hull (dried)
- Vodka OR apple cider vinegar
- Glass jar with lid

### Instructions
1. **Place** black walnut hull in glass jar
2. **Cover** with vodka or apple cider vinegar
3. **Seal** jar tightly
4. **Store** in warm place for **4 weeks**
5. **Strain** the fluid after 4 weeks
6. **Dosage:** 1 dropper full in glass of water daily
