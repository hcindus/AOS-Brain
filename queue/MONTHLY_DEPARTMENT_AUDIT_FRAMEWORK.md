## AUDIT WORKFLOW

### Week 1: Preparation
- **Jordan:** Sends audit notification to department leads
- **Patricia:** Confirms resources available
- **Departments:** Gather documentation

### Week 2: Audit Execution
- **Departments:** Complete audit template
- **Jordan:** Reviews submissions
- **Patricia:** Tracks completion

### Week 3: Review & Analysis
- **Jordan:** Compiles audit findings
- **Patricia:** Identifies compliance gaps
- **Captain:** Reviews if escalated

### Week 4: Action & Follow-Up
- **Departments:** Complete action items
- **Patricia:** Verifies completion
- **Jordan:** Archives audit reports

```bash

```

**7 Days Before Due:**

- Copy Jordan
- Include audit template

**3 Days Before Due:**
- Send escalation warning
- Alert if resources needed
- Offer assistance

**1 Day Before Due:**
- Final reminder
- Escalate to Captain if critical
- Prepare enforcement actions

**Day After Due (if missed):**
- Compliance violation logged
- Captain notified
- Remediation plan required

## ESCALATION PROCEDURES

### Level 1: Patricia Escalation
- Missed audit deadline
- Incomplete audit submission
- Non-compliance with action items

**Action:** Warning + Remediation plan required

### Level 2: Jordan Escalation
- Repeat violations
- Significant risks identified
- Resource conflicts

**Action:** Captain notification + Review meeting

### Level 3: Captain Escalation
- Critical security risks
- Legal compliance failures
- Department dysfunction

**Action:** Direct intervention + Restructuring

## METRICS & KPIs

### Department Compliance Score
```
Score = (Completed Audits / Total Audits) × 100

100% = Perfect compliance
90-99% = Minor issues (Patricia follow-up)
80-89% = Action required (Jordan review)
<80% = Critical (Captain intervention)
```

### Audit Quality Score
```
Score based on:
- Completeness of documentation
- Risk identification accuracy
- Action item clarity
- Timeliness of submission
```
