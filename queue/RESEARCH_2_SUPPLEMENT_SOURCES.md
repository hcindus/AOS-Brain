## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

### Suspicious Signs
❌ No certification mentioned  
❌ Extremely cheap prices  
❌ No expiration date  
❌ No contact information  
❌ Fake reviews  
❌ MLM/pyramid scheme structure  
❌ "Miracle cure" claims  
❌ No third-party testing

### Quality Certifications to Look For
✅ USDA Organic  
✅ NSF Certified  
✅ GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice)  
✅ USP Verified  
✅ Non-GMO Project  
✅ Third-party lab tested
