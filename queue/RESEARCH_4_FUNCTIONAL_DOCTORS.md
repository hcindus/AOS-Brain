## WHAT IS FUNCTIONAL MEDICINE?

Functional medicine addresses root causes of disease rather than just symptoms. For parasite treatment, they typically:
- Order comprehensive stool testing
- Review diet and lifestyle factors
- Prescribe targeted treatments (including off-label options)
- Monitor progress with follow-up testing
- Support gut healing post-treatment

## QUESTIONS TO ASK POTENTIAL DOCTORS

### Before Scheduling

**Testing Questions:**
1. "Do you order comprehensive parasite stool testing?"
2. "Which labs do you use?" (Look for: Doctor's Data, Genova, Diagnostic Solutions)
3. "Do you test for parasites beyond standard O&P?" (PCR, antigen testing)

**Treatment Questions:**
4. "Are you familiar with herbal parasite protocols?"
5. "Do you prescribe Ivermectin when appropriate?"
6. "What's your approach to gut healing post-treatment?"

**Philosophy Questions:**
7. "How do you approach root cause vs symptom management?"
8. "Do you support patient autonomy in treatment decisions?"
9. "What's your experience with parasite cases?"

**Practical Questions:**
10. "Do you accept insurance?"
11. "What's the typical cost for initial consultation?"
12. "How long until follow-up appointments?"

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

### 🚩 Warning Signs
- **"Miracle cure" claims**
- **No testing before treatment**
- **Pushes expensive supplements exclusively**
- **Dismisses your concerns**
- **No follow-up plan**
- **MLM supplement schemes**
- **Claims to cure everything**

### ✅ Good Signs
- Orders comprehensive testing FIRST
- Explains reasoning for treatments
- Respects patient autonomy
- Has clear follow-up protocol
- Works with your other doctors
- Evidence-based approach

## INSURANCE CONSIDERATIONS

### Typical Coverage

| Service | Usually Covered? | Notes |
|---------|------------------|-------|
| **Initial consultation** | Sometimes | May be out-of-network |
| **Lab testing** | Often | If ordered by MD |
| **Prescriptions** | Usually | Standard pharmacy coverage |
| **Supplements** | Rarely | Health savings accounts may apply |
| **Follow-ups** | Sometimes | Depends on plan |

### Cost-Saving Tips

1. **Health Savings Account (HSA)** - Tax-free dollars for functional medicine
2. **Flexible Spending Account (FSA)** - Pre-tax medical expenses
3. **Out-of-network benefits** - Submit receipts for reimbursement
4. **Sliding scale** - Some practitioners offer based on income
5. **Payment plans** - Ask about installment options

## RED FLAGS IN YOUR SYMPTOMS

### Seek Immediate Medical Care If:
- Severe abdominal pain
- Blood in stool
- High fever
- Severe dehydration
- Confusion
- Rapid weight loss
- Signs of anemia (extreme fatigue, pale skin)

**Go to ER or urgent care - don't wait for functional medicine appointment**
