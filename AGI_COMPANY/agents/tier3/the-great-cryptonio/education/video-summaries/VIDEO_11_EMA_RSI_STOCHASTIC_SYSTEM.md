## 📐 Part 3: The Full Strategy (EMA + RSI + Stochastic)

### Advantage #1: Trend Direction (EMAs)

**Bullish Trend Rules:**
- All 3 EMAs sloping **upward**
- Price **above** all 3 EMAs
- Order: 25 (top) → 75 (middle) → 140 (bottom)

**Bearish Trend Rules:**
- All 3 EMAs sloping **downward**
- Price **below** all 3 EMAs

**Critical Rule:**
- Trade **WITH** the trend
- Don't fight the current
- Sideways = NO TRADE

---

### Advantage #2: High-Probability Bounce Zones

**Pullback Behavior in Trend:**
- Markets don't move straight → they breathe
- Pullbacks = opportunity for pro traders
- Newbies panic → Pros prepare

**EMA Road Map in Bearish Trend (Example):**

| Sequence | Action | Result |
|----------|--------|--------|
| Price up → hits 25 EMA | First resistance | Bounce down |
| Breaks 25 → reaches 75 EMA | Second defense | Bounce down |
| Breaks 75 → reaches 140 EMA | Final barrier | Bounce down (trend continues) |
| Breaks 140 with no bounce | ⚠️ Warning | Trend may be reversing |

**Golden Rule:**
> "When price pulls back, don't freak out. Follow the EMA road map. First check 25. If breaks, watch 75. If breaks, 140 is final."

---

### Advantage #3: Trend Strength (RSI)

**RSI Settings:**
- Period: **75** (matches middle EMA)
- Critical level: **50**

**Rules:**
| RSI Position | Interpretation | Action |
|--------------|----------------|--------|
| RSI > 50 | Bullish strength | Look for long entries on pullbacks |
| RSI < 50 | Bearish strength | Look for short entries on bounces |
| RSI crossing 50 | Battlefield/neutral | Caution zone |

**The Golden Combo:**
- Price bounces off EMA line
- RSI confirms trend direction (>50 or <50)
- = High probability entry

**Example:**
- Bearish trend
- Price pulls up to 25/75/140 EMA
- RSI still below 50
- = Trend confirmed, look for sell

---

### Advantage #4: Entry Timing (Stochastic Oscillator)

**Prerequisites Before Using Stochastic:**
1. ✅ Trend confirmed by 3 EMAs
2. ✅ Trend strength confirmed by RSI
3. ❌ Stochastic ALONE = false signals

**Stochastic Settings:**
- Standard: **14,3,3** (globally visible = shared signals)
- Overbought zone: **80**
- Oversold zone: **20**

**Entry Rules:**

**For Bearish Trends (Shorts):**
- Wait for EMA bounce setup
- RSI < 50 (confirmation)
- Stochastic enters **overbought (80+)** AND crosses **down**
- = PERFECT SELL ENTRY

**For Bullish Trends (Longs):**
- Wait for EMA dip setup
- RSI > 50 (confirmation)
- Stochastic enters **oversold (20-)** AND crosses **up**
- = PERFECT BUY ENTRY

## 📐 Summary of Rules

### Entry Checklist (ALL must align):

✅ **Rule 1: Trend** (EMAs)
- 3 EMAs sloped in direction of trade
- Price on correct side of EMAs
- Pullback occurring toward EMAs

✅ **Rule 2: Strength** (RSI)
- RSI confirms trend direction
- RSI > 50 = bullish | RSI < 50 = bearish

✅ **Rule 3: Timing** (Stochastic)
- Stochastic in extreme zone (80+ or 20-)
- Cross in direction of trend
- NOT before EMA touch

✅ **Rule 4: Risk Management**
- SL above/below EMA being tested
- If EMA breaks, exit
- Target 15:1 to 10:1 R:R

- ❌ Price sideways (no trend)
- ❌ EMAs flat or crossed messily

**The Cost:**
- False signals
