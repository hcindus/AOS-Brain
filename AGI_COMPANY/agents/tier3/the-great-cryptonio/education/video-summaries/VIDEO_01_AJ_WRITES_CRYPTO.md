## Video 1: AJ Writes Crypto - How to Trade Crypto
**Source:** https://youtu.be/qZDAq_r1uu8  
**Length:** 26 minutes  
**Key Topics:** Risk management, confluence trading, stop losses, Fibonacci

### Core Concepts:

#### 1. Trading Philosophy
- **"Most people will lose money trading"** — due to impatience and lack of risk management
- **Successful trading = waiting for the perfect setup**, not trading daily
- **Turn $200k into $1M** by waiting for significant occurrences, not daily trades
- **You trade because an alarm went off**, not because you "feel like it"

#### 2. Trading Styles
| Style | Duration | Goal |
|-------|----------|------|
| **Scalp Trading** | 30 min - 2 hours | Quick profits within ranges |
| **Swing Trading** | Days - Weeks | Larger moves |
| **Intra-day** | Hours | Daily signals |

#### 3. Risk Management Rules
- **Set stop-losses** — prevents liquidation
- **Use isolated margin** (not cross) — limits risk to committed funds
- **Move stop-loss into profit** as trade goes your way — "trailing stop"
- **Leverage:** 3x (low confidence) → 6-7x (typical) → 9-10x (high confidence)
- **Never risk more than you can afford to lose**

#### 4. The Confluence Checklist
Before entering any trade, verify multiple indicators align:
- ✅ **Support/Resistance levels** — key accumulation zones
- ✅ **Momentum oscillators** — RSI, MACD, Market Cipher (volume + momentum)
- ✅ **Divergence** — price down while momentum up (bullish divergence)
- ✅ **EMAs/Moving Averages** — trend confirmation
- ✅ **Volume profiles** — confirmation of significance
- ✅ **Chart patterns** — breakouts, ranges, trends

#### 5. Key Technical Patterns

**Support/Resistance Levels:**
- Historical levels that act as accumulation (support) or rejection (resistance)
- Example: Algorand at 16 cents — buy zone, 103% gain in 53 days, 296% in 18 days
- When price breaks S/R, it's a "significant occurrence"

**Trend Lines:**
- Parallel channels showing uptrends/downtrends
- "Point of intersection" — where multiple trends converge (forced breakout)
- Set alerts on trend lines in TradingView

**Range Trading:**
- Identify consolidation ranges (e.g., Cardano in 19% range for 97 days)
- "Tightening the range" — smaller ranges within larger ranges
- Break of range = significant trade signal

**Breakout Trading:**
- Don't trade trend-line breakouts alone
- Wait for confirmation (support/resistance level confirmation)
- Example: Pepe broke downtrend + confirmed with S/R — 18% profit in 16 days

#### 6. Fibonacci Retracement
- Tool for finding support/resistance based on mathematical ratios
- **Pull from swing low to swing high**
- **Key levels:** 0.618 (golden ratio), 0.786 (deep retracement)
- **Accumulate under 0.618 or 0.786**
- Found in nature, markets, life — "magic numbers"

#### 7. Order Book Analysis
- Trade coins with high volume ($75M+ on Bit Unix)
- Avoid low-liquidity coins (e.g., Pi, Popcat with $21M)
- High liquidity = easier entry/exit for large positions

#### 8. Trade Execution Flow
1. Identify S/R levels on higher timeframe
2. Set alerts at key levels
3. When alarm triggers → check confluence checklist
4. Verify divergence, momentum, volume, EMAs
5. Determine position size ($1,000-$300,000 based on confidence)
6. Use isolated margin, set leverage (6-7x typical)
7. Set stop-loss above/below recent S/R
8. Move stop-loss into profit as trade progresses
9. Let winners run, cut losers quickly
