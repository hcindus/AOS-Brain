## 📊 VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price)

### What It Is:
**Volume Weighted Average Price = The average trading price of the DAY weighted by volume**

Think of it as: "What did most people pay today on average?"

### Key Psychology:
> "When people are up a lot then down a lot then even, what do they do? They usually buy more or sell. VWAP gives you that edge — this is the market's average."

### Core Rules:

| Position | Action |
|----------|--------|
| **Price ABOVE VWAP** | Can take CALLS / Longs ONLY |
| **Price BELOW VWAP** | Can take PUTS / Shorts ONLY |
| **Never opposite** | Never calls below, never puts above |

## 📊 8/9 EMA (Exponential Moving Average)

### Two Uses:

#### Use #1: Holding "Runners" (Primary Use)
**Rule:**
- In a trade (long or short)
- Hold position as long as price stays above/below EMA
- Exit when price **closes** on opposite side of EMA

**Example (Long Trade):**
```
Entry: VWAP retest, above EMA
Hold:  → Riding EMA up
        → Riding EMA up
        → Riding EMA up
        ⚠️ Closes under EMA = EXIT
                ↓
        Take profits
```

**The Magic:** EMA acts as a "trailing stop loss" based on last 8 candles of price action

#### Use #2: EMA Retest Scalps
- Trade retests of EMA itself
- Smaller, quicker scalps
- Less reliance than VWAP retests

## 🎯 The Strategy — 3 Simple Rules

### Rule 1: Direction (VWAP Position)
- Price must be on correct side of VWAP for trade direction
- Above = Long only, Below = Short only

### Rule 2: Entry (VWAP Retest)
- Wait for price to retest VWAP
- Must be on correct side of VWAP
- Bonus: Aligns with pre-market high/low

### Rule 3: Exit (EMA Close)
- Hold until price closes on opposite side of EMA
- EMA = trailing stop

## 📐 VWAP + Pre-Market Level Logic

### Why This Matters:

**Scenario 1: VWAP UNDER Pre-Market Low**
```
Price → Drops to VWAP
         ↓
   VWAP (under pre-market low)
         ↓
    If breaks VWAP = can't take puts
    VWAP retest = take puts (not PM low retest)
```

**Scenario 2: VWAP ABOVE Pre-Market High**
```
Price → Pulls back to VWAP
         ↓
   VWAP (above pre-market high)  
         ↓
    VWAP retest = take calls
    If breaks VWAP = invalid
```

**The Rule:**
- VWAP determines if trade is valid
- Pre-market levels determine entry exact price
- Combined = massive confluence

## 📊 Technical Deep Dive

### VWAP Mechanics:
- **Calculation:** Σ(Price × Volume) / Σ(Volume)
- **Reset:** Daily (or can be anchored)
- **Behavior:** 
  - Strong stocks = ride above VWAP all day
  - Weak stocks = reject VWAP, trend below
  - Choppy = cross VWAP multiple times (avoid)

### EMA Mechanics (8/9 period):
- **Calculation:** Exponential weighted average
- **Lag:** Minimal (very responsive)
- **Best timeframe:** 5-minute chart (shown in video)
- **Behavior:**
  - Trending stock = price "rides" EMA
  - Breaking EMA = momentum shift
  - Closing outside = exit signal
