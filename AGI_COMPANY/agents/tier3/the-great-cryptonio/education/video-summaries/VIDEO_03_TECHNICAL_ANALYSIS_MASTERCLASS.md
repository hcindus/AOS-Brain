## CHAPTER 2: Basics of Reading Charts

### Candlestick Anatomy
```
    HIGH
     |
   [BODY] ← Open to Close
     |
    LOW

BODY = Open → Close (filled = red/bear, empty = green/bull)
WICKS = Range beyond body (shows rejection)
```

### Supply and Demand Fundamentals
- **Price UP** = More DEMAND than supply
- **Price DOWN** = More SUPPLY than demand
- **Consolidation/Sideways** = Supply = Demand (waiting for catalyst)
- **Trends** = Sustained supply/demand imbalance

### Core Trading Philosophies
| Strategy | Approach | Best For |
|----------|----------|----------|
| **Continuation** | Early in trend, ride direction | Strong trends |
| **Reversal** | End of trend, counter-trend exhaustion | Confirmed reversals |

## CHAPTER 3: Trends/Support & Resistance

### Trend Definitions

#### UPTREND
```
Higher High → Higher Low → Higher High → Higher Low → Higher High
    🔺         🔻            🔺            🔻            🔺
```
- **Minimum:** 2 higher highs + 2 higher lows (4 candles)
- **Break of Structure:** Continuation above previous high
- **Change of Character:** Break below previous higher low (potential reversal)

#### DOWNTREND
```
Lower Low → Lower High → Lower Low → Lower High
   🔻          🔺           🔻          🔺
```
- **Minimum:** 2 lower lows + 2 lower highs (4 candles)
- **Break of Structure:** Continuation below previous low
- **Change of Character:** Break above previous high (potential reversal)

#### CONSOLIDATION
- Neither uptrend nor downtrend established
- Sideways price action
- Waiting for imbalance/supply-demand catalyst

### Key Terms
| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Impulse Wave** | Movement IN direction of trend |
| **Corrective Wave** | Movement AGAINST trend |
| **Break of Structure** | Breaking previous high/low IN trend direction |
| **Change of Character** | Breaking structure AGAINST trend (reversal sign) |

## CHAPTER 4: Trend Market Structure

### Elliott Wave Theory

#### The 5-3 Wave Pattern
```
TREND (Impulse)                    CORRECTION
   3                                  ┌───b───┐
  / \                                 │       │
 /   \ 5                            a         c
2     \  📈                      📈──┘       └──📈
 \   /                              
  \ /                                 
   1────4
       \
         \
```

**Impulse Wave (5 moves):**
1. Wave 1 — Initial move
2. Wave 2 — Correction
3. Wave 3 — Strongest move (ALWAYS exceeds Wave 1)
4. Wave 4 — Correction (NEVER below Wave 1 high)
5. Wave 5 — Final impulse

**Correction (3 moves):**
- A — First pullback
- B — Counter-trend attempt
- C — Final correction

### Elliott Wave Rules
1. **Wave 3 CANNOT be shortest wave** — if shorter than Wave 1, expect extended Wave 5
2. **Alternation Rule:** If Wave 2 is complex (choppy), Wave 4 will be simple (straight), and vice versa
3. **Wave 4 cannot consolidate below Wave 1 high** — if it does, invalidates momentum

### Fibonacci Extensions (Golden Ratio)
**Key Levels:** 1.618, 2.618, 3.618

**How to Draw:**
1. Click at trend start
2. Drag to Wave 1 high
3. Return to trend start
4. Extensions appear automatically

**What It Means:**
- 1.618 = 161.8% of Wave 1 distance
- 2.618 = 261.8% of Wave 1 distance
- These levels often mark trend exhaustion/reversal points

### Fibonacci Retracements
**Key Levels:** 23.6%, 38.2%, 50%, 61.8%, 78.6%

**How to Draw:**
1. Click at trend low
2. Drag to trend high
3. Levels show potential pullback areas

**Critical Levels:**
- **61.8%** (Golden Ratio) — Most common continuation point
- **78.6%** — Deep retracement, near invalidation
- **50%** — Psychological level, common support

## CHAPTER 5: General Price Action Patterns

### U-Shape vs V-Shape

#### U-Shape (Cup)
- Gradual selloff that curves back up
- Buyers maintain interest
- **Signal:** Higher probability of continuation
- Often followed by "handle" consolidation

#### V-Shape
- Sharp drop, immediate attempt to reclaim
- Weak pullback
- **Signal:** Short-lived move, likely rejection

### Triangles (Symmetrical/Converging)
```
    /\
   /  \
  /    \
 /      \
──────────
```
- **Setup:** Higher lows + lower highs converging
- **Breakout:** Continuation in direction of break
- **Target:** Measure width of triangle, project from break

### Cup and Handle
- **Cup:** U-shaped rounded bottom
- **Handle:** Small downward consolidation
- **Breakout:** Above handle resistance
- **Target:** Depth of cup projected upward

## CHAPTER 6: Smart Money Concepts I Use

### Fair Value Gaps (FVG) ⭐ CRITICAL

#### Definition:
3-candle pattern where middle candle's body doesn't overlap wicks of candles 1 and 3

```
   Candle 1        Candle 2        Candle 3
      |               ☐               |
    _/|\_         _/  |  \_         _/|\_
   /  |  \       /    |    \       /  |  \
      |            GAP ZONE            |
      |               |                |
```

**The Gap Zone** = Fair Value Gap (imbalance area)

#### How Price Responds:
- Price often returns to FVG to "fill the imbalance"
- Can act as magnetic support/resistance
- **Bullish FVG:** Gap below candles (demand zone)
- **Bearish FVG:** Gap above candles (supply zone)

#### Trading FVG:
1. Identify gap after strong move
2. Wait for price to return to gap
3. Enter on reaction from gap
4. Stop loss: Beyond the gap

### Break of Structure (BOS)
- Breaking previous high/low IN trend direction
- Confirms trend continuation
- **Bullish BOS:** Price takes out previous high
- **Bearish BOS:** Price takes out previous low

### Change of Character (ChoCH)
- Breaking structure AGAINST trend direction
- First sign of potential reversal
- **Bullish ChoCH:** Break above previous high in downtrend
- **Bearish ChoCH:** Break below previous low in uptrend

### Order Blocks / Liquidity Zones
- Areas where large orders were filled
- Often act as future support/resistance
- **Bullish Order Block:** Last bearish candle before strong move up
- **Bearish Order Block:** Last bullish candle before strong move down

## Volume Analysis

### Volume Indicators on TradingView:
1. **Standard Volume** (bars at bottom)
2. **Moving Average on Volume** (20-period default)

### How to Read Volume:
- **Volume ↑ + Price ↑** = Confirmed trend (healthy)
- **Volume ↓ + Price ↑** = Trend may fade (exhaustion)
- **Volume ↑ + Price ↓** = Confirmed downtrend (panic selling)
- **Volume ↑ on reversal** = New trend beginning

**Key Concept:** Volume = Market participation/conviction
