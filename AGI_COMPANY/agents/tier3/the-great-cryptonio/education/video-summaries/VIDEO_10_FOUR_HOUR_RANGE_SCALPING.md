## 📐 The Strategy: 4-Hour Range

### What is the 4-Hour Range?
The range between the **high and low of the first 4-hour candle** that forms during the day (New York time).

**Why New York Time?**
- Traditional forex/crypto market opens align with NY business hours
- First 4H candle = 12:00 AM - 4:00 AM EST
- Sets the daily range baseline

---

## 📝 Three-Step Checklist

### Step 1: Mark the Range (4H Chart)

**Setup:**
1. Open chart (any asset)
2. Set timeframe to **4 hours**
3. Change timezone to **New York time** (bottom right TradingView)
4. Find the **first 4H candle of today**
   - Example: Today is June 23 → Find June 23's first 4H candle
5. Draw horizontal lines at:
   - **High** of that candle
   - **Low** of that candle
6. Extend both lines to end of day

**Important:** Wait for candle to **fully close** before marking!

---

### Step 2: Find Setup (5-Minute Chart)

**Transition:**
1. Drop down to **5-minute timeframe**
2. Look for breakout + re-entry pattern

**Valid Setup Pattern:**
```
1. 5M candle CLOSES outside range (above high OR below low)
   - Note: Wicks don't count, needs full body close
   
2. Wait for price to re-enter range
   - Must CLOSE back INSIDE the range
   
3. Must occur SAME DAY as marked 4H range
```

**Visual:**
```
Range High ────────────────────
                  │    [Breakout]
                  │         │
                  │    ←────── Re-enter (CLOSE inside)
                  │         │
Range Low ────────┴──────────────
                   [5M chart movement]
```

---

### Step 3: Entry Execution

**Long Setup (Bullish):**
- **Trigger:** Price breaks BELOW range low → Re-enters/closes back inside
- **Entry:** On closing back inside range
- **Stop Loss:** At the exact low of the breakout move
- **Take Profit:** 2x the stop-loss distance (2R)

**Short Setup (Bearish):**
- **Trigger:** Price breaks ABOVE range high → Re-enters/closes back inside
- **Entry:** On closing back inside range
- **Stop Loss:** At the exact high of the breakout move
- **Take Profit:** 2x the stop-loss distance (2R)

## 🔧 Advanced Adjustment

### When Breakout is "Too Large"

**Problem:** Large breakout = Massive stop-loss distance

**Solution:** Use nearest key level instead of exact breakout point

**Example 1 (Bitcoin):**
- Breakout went very far below range low
- Regular SL: 200+ pips away
- Adjusted SL: Placed at nearby resistance level (~50 pips)
- Result: Still achieved 2R profit

**Example 2 (Gold):**
- Large breakout below
- Regular SL: Too far
- Adjusted SL: At nearby order block
- Result: Valid 2R profit

**Key Levels to Use:**
- Recent support/resistance
- Order blocks
- Previous day high/low
- Psychological levels

## ⏰ Time Management

### Daily Reset:
- Range is valid **only for same day**
- New day = New first 4H candle = New range
- All setups must occur within that trading day

### Multiple Trades per Day:
- **Valid:** Yes, as long as within same day's range
- Each breakout + re-entry = New setup
- Maximize opportunity during active hours

### End of Day:
- If price doesn't re-enter → No trade
- Reset at 12:00 AM NY time next day

---

## 🎯 Risk Management

### Fixed Rules:
1. **Always 2R take profit** (2x stop loss)
2. **Never adjust SL** (unless breakout too large)
3. **Wait for close** (no anticipation)
4. **Same day only** (respect time boundary)

### R-Multiple Tracking:
- Win = +2R
- Loss = -1R
- Risk:Reward = 1:2

### Required Win Rate:
- With 1:2 R:R, need **~34% win rate** to break even
- Strategy achieved 60-83% across markets
- **Edge = Risk management + high probability setups**
