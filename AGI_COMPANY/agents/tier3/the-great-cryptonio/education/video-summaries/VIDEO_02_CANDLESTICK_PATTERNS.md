## Pattern 1: The Engulfing Candle ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** HIGH  
**Type:** Reversal pattern

### Bullish Engulfing
- **Appearance:** Green candle completely engulfs previous red candle
- **Location:** At strong support level
- **Signal:** Sellers exhausted, buyers taking control
- **Entry:** After engulfing candle closes

### Bearish Engulfing  
- **Appearance:** Red candle completely engulfs previous green candle
- **Location:** At strong resistance level
- **Signal:** Buyers exhausted, sellers taking control
- **Entry:** After engulfing candle closes

**Important Note:** Use with support/resistance, not in isolation. Confluence critical.

## Pattern 2: The Momentum Candle ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** HIGH  
**Type:** Trend continuation/breakout

### Characteristics:
- **Size:** 2-3x larger than previous candles
- **Color:** Indicates direction (green = up, red = down)
- **Location:** After choppy/sideways markets

### Why It Works:
- Sideways markets trap shorters with stop losses above support
- Momentum candle hits liquidation zones, triggering chain reaction
- Breaks consolidation, starts new trend

### Best Use:
- **Long entry:** Big green candle after choppy market
- **Short entry:** Big red candle after choppy market
- **Profit potential:** Trend continuation for extended moves

## Pattern 3: Multiple Candlestick Pattern (Wick Analysis) ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** MEDIUM-HIGH  
**Type:** Support/resistance confirmation

### Bullish Setup:
- **Appearance:** 3+ candles with wicks pointing down at support
- **Meaning:** Sellers trying to break support, buyers fighting back
- **Confirmation:** More candles = better probability

### Bearish Setup:
- **Appearance:** 3+ candles with wicks pointing up at resistance  
- **Meaning:** Buyers trying to break resistance, sellers rejecting
- **Confirmation:** More candles = better probability

**Key:** Shows battle between buyers and sellers — wicks = rejection.

## Pattern 4: The Doji Candle ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** MEDIUM  
**Type:** Indecision/reversal

### Characteristics:
- **Body:** Very thin (open ≈ close)
- **Wicks:** Can appear on either or both sides
- **Meaning:** Market uncertain, potential reversal imminent

### Trading Strategy:
1. See Doji after trend
2. Wait for 2+ confirmatory candles (same color)
3. Enter after confirmation

### Variations:
| Type | Appearance | Signal |
|------|------------|--------|
| **Regular Doji** | Thin body, small wicks | Reversal possible |
| **Long-Legged** | Thin body, long wicks | High volatility indecision |
| **Dragonfly** | Small body, long bottom wick | Bullish reversal at bottom |
| **Gravestone** | Small body, long upper wick | Bearish reversal at top |

## Pattern 5: The Hammer ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** HIGH  
**Type:** Bullish reversal

### Appearance:
- **Body:** Decent size
- **Wick:** Long bottom wick (2-3x body size)
- **Location:** At support/after downtrend

### What It Means:
- Sellers pushed price way down
- Buyers absorbed ALL selling pressure
- Price recovered back up, closing near open
- **Extremely bullish signal**

### Entry:
- After hammer closes at support
- Confirm with follow-through candle

---

## Pattern 6: The Shooting Star ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** HIGH  
**Type:** Bearish reversal

### Appearance:
- **Body:** Decent size
- **Wick:** Long upper wick (2-3x body size)
- **Location:** At resistance/after uptrend

### What It Means:
- Buyers pushed price way up
- Sellers absorbed ALL buying pressure
- Price fell back down, closing near open
- **Extremely bearish signal**

### Entry:
- After shooting star closes at resistance
- Mirror image of hammer

---

## Pattern 7: The Tweezer Pattern ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** MEDIUM-HIGH  
**Type:** Reversal (2-candle pattern)

### Bullish Tweezer:
- **First:** Red candle with bottom wick
- **Second:** Green candle with bottom wick (same level)
- **Location:** At support
- **Meaning:** Price hitting same low twice, rejecting it both times

### Bearish Tweezer:
- **First:** Green candle with upper wick
- **Second:** Red candle with upper wick (same level)
- **Location:** At resistance
- **Meaning:** Price hitting same high twice, rejecting it

### Entry:
- After second candle confirms pattern
- Good for support/resistance bounces

---

## Pattern 8: The Marubozu ⭐ PRIORITY
**Reliability:** HIGH  
**Type:** Trend continuation

### Bullish Marubozu:
- **Appearance:** Large green rectangle, NO WICKS
- **Meaning:** Buyers have COMPLETE control
- **Location:** Middle of uptrend
- **Signal:** Trend will continue

### Bearish Marubozu:
- **Appearance:** Large red rectangle, NO WICKS  
- **Meaning:** Sellers have COMPLETE control
- **Location:** Middle of downtrend
- **Signal:** Trend will continue

### Physics:
- **No upper wick:** Open was the low (bullish) OR close was the high (bearish)
- **No lower wick:** Open was the high (bearish) OR close was the low (bullish)
- **Full control by one side**
