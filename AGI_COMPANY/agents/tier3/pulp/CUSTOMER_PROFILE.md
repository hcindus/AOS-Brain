## 🎯 YOUR TARGET CUSTOMERS

**Industry:** Food Service & Hospitality  
**Business Types:** Five core segments

### 1. **CAFES** ☕
**Profile:**
- Coffee shops, espresso bars, tea houses
- Morning rush focused (6am-11am peak)
- Equipment needs: Espresso machines, grinders, refrigeration
- Pain point: Equipment downtime during morning rush = lost revenue
- Decision maker: Owner/Manager
- Average order: $2,000-$15,000

**Sales Angle:** "Keep your morning rush running. Reliable equipment = happy customers."

---

### 2. **BURGER JOINTS** 🍔
**Profile:**
- Fast casual burger spots, diners with burgers
- High-volume kitchens, quick turnaround
- Equipment needs: Grills, fryers, ventilation, POS systems
- Pain point: Kitchen speed during lunch/dinner rushes
- Decision maker: Owner/Operator
- Average order: $5,000-$25,000

**Sales Angle:** "Speed is money. Equipment that keeps up with your rushes."

---

### 3. **RESTAURANTS** (Full Service) 🍽️
**Profile:**
- Sit-down dining, varied menus
- Kitchen complexity: High (multiple stations)
- Equipment needs: Ranges, ovens, dishwashers, coolers, prep tables
- Pain point: Consistent quality, kitchen coordination
- Decision maker: Owner/Executive Chef
- Average order: $10,000-$50,000+

**Sales Angle:** "Your kitchen runs on reliable equipment. We keep it running."

---

### 4. **QUICK SERVICE RESTAURANTS (QSR)** ⚡
**Profile:**
- Fast food, fast casual, takeout-focused
- Speed is EVERYTHING
- Equipment needs: POS systems, fryers, warming cabinets, drink stations
- Pain point: Throughput during peak hours
- Decision maker: Franchisee/Manager (often corporate standards)
- Average order: $15,000-$75,000

**Sales Angle:** "Speed and reliability. Equipment that matches your pace."

---

### 5. **FINE DINING** 🥂
**Profile:**
- Upscale restaurants, high-end service
- Presentation matters, precise temperature control
- Equipment needs: High-end ranges, wine coolers, specialized prep equipment
- Pain point: Consistency, precision, ambiance
- Decision maker: Owner/Executive Chef (picky buyers)
- Average order: $20,000-$100,000+

**Sales Angle:** "Precision equipment for precision dining."
