## 🎯 THE TACTICAL SEQUENCE

### **Step 1: Open with Supplies** 🏷️
**Lead Product:** Register rolls or Phenol-Free thermal paper

**Opening Line:**
> "How much are you paying for register rolls right now? I can save you 20% and deliver monthly."

**Why:**
- ✅ Low friction — easy yes
- ✅ Everyone needs it
- ✅ Recurring revenue
- ✅ Builds trust

---

### **Step 2: Deliver Consistently** 📦
**Monthly Bundles:**
- Register rolls
- Thermal paper (Phenol-Free)

**Value Prop:** Save 20%, never run out, free delivery
