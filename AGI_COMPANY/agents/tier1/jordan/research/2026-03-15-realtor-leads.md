# Realtor Lead Sources for CREAM CRM

**Date:** 2026-03-15  
**Product:** CREAM ($699 Real Estate CRM)  
**Target:** Realtors, Brokers, Real Estate Agents

---

## Primary Data Sources

### 1. California-Specific (Leverage CA SOS Scraper)

| Source | Access Method | Cost | Notes |
|----------|---------------|------|-------|
| **CA DRE License Database** | Public records via DRE.ca.gov | Free | 400k+ active licensees. Scrape by county/region |
| **CAR (California Association of Realtors)** | Membership directory | Member-only | 200k+ members. Partner or sponsor for access |
| **Regional MLS Systems** | CRMLS, SFARMLS, etc. | Varies | CoreLogic, Bright MLS. Broker partnerships required |
| **Local Realtor Boards** | Bay Area, LA, SD boards | Free-$500 | Event sponsorships get attendee lists |

### 2. National Lead Generation Services

| Service | Pricing | Quality | Best For |
|---------|---------|---------|----------|
| **Zillow Premier Agent** | $200-500/lead | High intent | Active buyers/sellers |
| **Realtor.com Pro** | Pay-per-lead | Medium | Volume play |
| **Redfin Partner Agents** | Referral fees | High | Established agents |
| **Offrs** | $0.50-2/lead | Predictive | FSBO, expired listings |
| **Vulcan7** | $99-299/mo | High | Expireds, FSBOs, FRBOs |
| **Mojo Dialer** | $99/mo | Medium | Cold calling lists |
| **Landvoice** | $67-147/mo | Medium | Expired/FSBO data |

### 3. MLS Database Access

**Direct MLS Access (requires broker license):**
- Bright MLS (Northeast/Mid-Atlantic)
- CRMLS (California)
- MLS Listings (Texas)
- Stellar MLS (Southeast)

**Workarounds without license:**
- Partner with licensed broker
- Use MLS data aggregators (CoreLogic, ATTOM)
- Purchase from list brokers (verify CAN-SPAM compliance)

### 4. Association & Event Lists

- **NAR Annual Conference** (20k+ attendees)
- **Inman Connect** (5k+ agents/brokers)
- **Local board events** (sponsor = attendee data)
- **Real estate meetups** (Meetup.com groups)

### 5. Scrapable Public Sources

| Source | Data Available | Scraping Difficulty |
|--------|----------------|---------------------|
| Realtor.com agent finder | Name, office, phone | Medium (anti-bot) |
| Zillow agent profiles | Reviews, sales history | Hard |
| LinkedIn (Sales Navigator) | 3M+ real estate pros | Easy with tools |
| Yelp real estate agents | Contact info | Easy |
| Chamber of Commerce | Local brokerages | Easy |
