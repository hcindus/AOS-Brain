## 4️⃣ OPTIMIZATION: Meeting Scheduling Automation

### Problem
Manual meeting scheduling with no conflict detection. No optimal time finding.

### Solution
Implemented **Automated Meeting Scheduler** with:
- **Conflict detection** - checks agent availability across schedule
- **Optimal time finding** - searches next 48 hours in 15-min increments
- **Role-based attendee selection** - Standup needs Technical+Management
- **Platform selection** - Strategy in Gather, Brainstorm in Minecraft

### Meeting Types
| Type | Duration | Platform | Attendees |
|------|----------|----------|-----------|
| Standup | 15 min | Gather | Technical + Management |
| Strategy | 60 min | Gather | C-suite + Management |
| Brainstorm | 45 min | Minecraft | Technical + Research + C-suite |
| Social | 30 min | Roblox | All agents |

### Scheduling Logic
```
1. Determine required roles from meeting type
2. Select available agents (meetings <5 per agent)
3. Search for time slot in next 48 hours
4. Block time for all attendees
5. Return meeting object with agenda
```

### Results
Meetings auto-schedule every 100 ticks without conflicts. Average 3-5 meetings per day optimally distributed.
