## 🔄 R&D PROCESS

### Phase 1: DISCOVER (Weeks 1-2)
**LAB leads**
- Market research
- Customer interviews
- Competitor analysis
- Output: Validated problem statement

### Phase 2: DESIGN (Weeks 3-4)
**PIXEL leads**
- User journey mapping
- Wireframes and mockups
- Technical feasibility check with BUILD
- Output: Design specs, prototype plan

### Phase 3: BUILD (Weeks 5-8)
**BUILD leads**
- MVP development
- Rapid iteration
- Daily standups
- Output: Working prototype

### Phase 4: TEST (Weeks 9-10)
**TEST leads**
- User testing
- A/B experiments
- Metrics analysis
- Output: Validation report

### Phase 5: PITCH (Week 11)
**PITCH leads**
- Business case
- Pricing strategy
- Sales deck
- Output: Go/no-go recommendation

### Phase 6: HANDOFF (Week 12+)
**PROTO coordinates**
- Transfer to production (Technical)
- Marketing campaign (Marketing)
- Sales training (Sales)
- Output: Launched product
