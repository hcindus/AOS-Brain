## Quick Tips for Email Follow-Ups

| Do This | Avoid This |
|---------|------------|
| Personalize the subject line | Generic "Following up" subjects |
| Reference specific details from your conversation | Copy-paste templates without customization |
| Offer value in every touchpoint | Asking "just checking in" repeatedly |
| Give prospects an easy out | Being pushy or guilt-tripping |
| Keep emails concise (under 125 words) | Long walls of text |
| Use one clear call-to-action | Multiple competing asks |
