## 📋 Pricing Playbook

### When Quoted Higher Than Competitor:

1. **Acknowledge**: *"I see their price is lower on this item"*
2. **Reframe**: *"But let's look at total monthly spend and what you get..."*
3. **Calculate**: Show TCO including labor, emergencies, stock-outs
4. **Demonstrate**: AGI features that save time/money
5. **Close**: *"So while we're $5 more on paper, you're saving $90 overall. Plus you get automation that eliminates the work."

### When Customer Is Price-Sensitive:

1. **Start Small**: *"Let's try us on just receipt paper for 90 days"*
2. **Prove Value**: Show AGI automation in action
3. **Expand**: *"Now that you've seen how this works, let's talk about your other supplies..."*

### When Competing on Large RFP:

1. **Lead with AGI**: No competitor can match this
2. **Bundle aggressively**: Cross-subsidize to win
3. **Offer pilot**: Lower risk for procurement
4. **Provide references**: Similar deployments
