# Performance Supply Depot - Collections Letter
**Type:** Formal Collections / Final Demand
**Purpose:** Last resort for seriously past-due accounts
**Warning:** FDCPA-compliant language required. Use carefully.

---

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT LEGAL NOTICE

These templates follow FDCPA (Fair Debt Collection Practices Act) guidelines:
- No harassment or threats
- No false representations
- Must identify as debt collector
- Must provide verification of debt
- Must cease contact if requested

**Before sending:** Verify debt amount, review account history, consider payment plan.

---

## Usage Guidelines

| Stage | Days Past Due | Template | Channel |
|-------|--------------|----------|---------|
| Friendly | 1-7 | Payment Reminder A | Email |
| Reminder | 7-14 | Payment Reminder B | Email + Call |
| Firm | 14-30 | Payment Reminder C/D | Email + Certified Mail |
| Formal | 30-45 | Collections A | Certified Mail |
| Final | 45-60 | Collections B | Certified Mail |
| Legal | 60+ | Attorney Review Required | Through Counsel |

---

**⚠️ Always consult legal counsel before sending formal collections letters.**

**Documentation:** Keep copies of all communications, proof of delivery, payment history.

**FDCPA Compliance:** Never threaten action you can't/won't take. Never harass. Always identify yourself.
