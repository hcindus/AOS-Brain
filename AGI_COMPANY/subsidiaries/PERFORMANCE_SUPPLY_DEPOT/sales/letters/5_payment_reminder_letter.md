# Performance Supply Depot - Payment Reminder Letter
**Type:** Past Due / Collections (Pre-Formal)
**Purpose:** Professional reminder without damaging relationship
**Tone:** Friendly → Firm → Formal

---

## Template A: Friendly Reminder (1-3 Days Past Due)

Subject: Quick reminder — Invoice #[Number]

Hi [First Name],

Hope you're doing well! Just wanted to follow up on Invoice #[Number] for $[Amount].

**Original Due Date:** [Date]
**Days Past Due:** [X]
**Amount:** $[Amount]

I know things get busy — no stress. If you already sent payment, ignore this. If not, any of these work:

- **Online:** [Payment Link] (30 seconds)
- **Phone:** [Phone]
- **Reply:** "Paying [Date]" and I'll note it

Thanks!
[Your Name]

---

**Invoice:** #[Number] — $[Amount]
