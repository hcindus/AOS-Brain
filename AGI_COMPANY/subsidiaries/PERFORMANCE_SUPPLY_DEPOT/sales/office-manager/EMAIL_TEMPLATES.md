## 📝 Email Best Practices

**Subject Lines:**
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use personalization ([First Name], [Company Name])
- Create curiosity or urgency
- Avoid spam triggers (FREE, !!!, ALL CAPS)

**Body:**
- Keep under 150 words for cold emails
- Use bullet points for readability
- One clear CTA per email
- Mobile-friendly formatting

**Timing:**
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
- Best times: 8-10 AM or 2-4 PM (prospect's timezone)
- Avoid Mondays and Fridays

**Follow-Up Sequence:**
1. Day 0: Initial outreach
2. Day 3: Follow-up #1
3. Day 7: Follow-up #2
4. Day 14: Follow-up #3
5. Day 30: Breakup email
