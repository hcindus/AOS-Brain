## ⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY DISCLAIMER

**READ BEFORE PURCHASING OR BUILDING**

### Physical Safety
- **These robots contain moving parts** that can pinch, crush, or strike
- **Servos are powerful** and can cause injury if fingers/hair/clothing caught
- **Balance systems are experimental** - robots CAN and WILL fall
- **Sharp edges** from 3D printed parts can cause cuts
- **Electrical hazard** - LiPo batteries can catch fire if damaged
- **Not a toy** - Recommended for ages 16+ with adult supervision

### Build Safety
- **3D printers** have hot surfaces (200°C+) that cause burns
- **Soldering** required - hot irons cause burns
- **Power tools** may be needed for assembly
- **Chemical exposure** - paints, solvents, adhesives

### Operational Safety
- **Never leave charging unattended** - fire risk
- **Keep away from children and pets**
- **Do not operate near stairs, ledges, or water**
- **Maximum safe speed:** Walking pace only
- **Emergency stop required** - build must include kill switch
