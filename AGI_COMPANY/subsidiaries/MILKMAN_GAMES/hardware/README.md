## Print Settings Recommendations

### General Settings
- **Layer Height:** 0.2mm (standard) or 0.12mm (high detail)
- **Infill:** 15-20% (grid or gyroid pattern)
- **Supports:** Required for overhangs >45° (spill_monster, straw_powerup)
- **Bed Adhesion:** Brim recommended for small parts (coin, cream_bonus)

### Material Recommendations
| Model | Recommended Filament | Notes |
|-------|---------------------|-------|
| Milkman Hero | White PLA | Classic milkman look |
| Milk Bottles | White or Opaque PLA | Use gold silk for golden_milk |
| Coin | Gold or Yellow PLA | Silk PLA for shine |
| Sour Milk Enemy | Sour Apple Green PLA | Or use white with green paint |
| Spill Monster | Blue or White PLA | Translucent blue looks great! |
| Straw Powerup | Red or Striped PLA | Consider multi-color print |
| Cream Bonus | White PLA | Add layer lines for texture |
| Milk Carton Crate | Cardboard Brown PLA | Or wood-filled PLA |

### Color Coding (Optional Painting)
If printing in one color, consider painting details:
- **Hero:** White base, red/blue accents for outfit
- **Enemies:** Green tint with angry faces
- **Powerups:** Bright colors (red straws, white cream)

## Assembly Notes

These models are designed to be:
- **Single-piece prints** - No assembly required
- **Balanced** - Flat base for stable standing
- **Kid-friendly** - No sharp edges, rounded corners
